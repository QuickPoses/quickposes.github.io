# Privacy Notice

Effective Date: May 27, 2020
Last Updated Date: Nov 3, 2025

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for **Quick Poses iOS application** and **[website](https://quickposes.app/)**. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by these Apps, except where stated otherwise. It will notify you of the following:
  
## What information we collect:  

The iOS app collects minimal data necessary for in-app purchase management through Purchasely (see In-App Purchases section below). The website requires no cookie or any tracking mechanism.

The iOS app accesses its own folder for getting the pose galleries and saving the user sessions results. No other access is used or needed by the app.

The service provider may automatically collect data related to crashes and statistics related to the User's use of the iOS app in a personally identifiable form.

TestFlight is a beta testing service provided by Apple Inc.

Personal Data processed: app information; Data communicated while using the service; device information; device logs; email address; first name; last name; Usage Data.

## In-App Purchases

This type of service enables the management of in-app purchases for the iOS application.

The service provider processes data necessary for the technical and operational management of purchase transactions. **We have explicitly enabled only the essential processing (Processing no.1) and have revoked all other processing options.**

### Purchasely (Purchasely SAS)

Purchasely is a purchase management service provided by Purchasely SAS.

**Important**: Quick Poses offers only one-time in-app purchases (no subscriptions). As such, no subscription-related data is collected or processed by Purchasely for this application.

Personal Data processed for essential processing (Processing no.1 only):
- Device identifiers (device ID): device-specific identifier generated randomly upon first initialization
- Anonymous identifier: randomly generated identifier for managing anonymous purchases without a user account
- App store receipts: transaction receipts generated by the mobile app stores
- Transaction data: purchase history for one-time in-app purchases from mobile app stores
- Technical information: device model, device name, OS, OS version, language, app version, app store country, smartphone manufacturer
- Purchase events: transaction events related to one-time purchases

**We explicitly revoke all other processing operations offered by Purchasely.** No subscription lifecycle data, subscription status, renewal information, or any subscription-related personal data is collected, as the application does not offer subscription services.

The device identifier and anonymous identifier may be stored locally on your device in the application's internal storage space without any retention limit.

Personal Data retention: Data is retained for the duration of the agreement between Quick Poses and Purchasely, and kept for five additional years as intermediate archiving to maintain proof of rights or obligations. Technical log data (from third-party processors like Datadog and Sentry) are kept for 30 days.

Place of processing: European Union, United States – [Data Processing Agreement](https://www.purchasely.com/hubfs/PURCHASELY-DATA-PROCESSING-AGREEMENT.pdf).
